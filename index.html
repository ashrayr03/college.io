Affiliation and Recognition

Approved, affiliated, and built for technical excellence

JNNCE is affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belagavi, approved by AICTE, recognized by the Government of Karnataka, and accredited by NBA and NAAC. In 2019, the institution was renamed Jawaharlal Nehru New College of Engineering following regulatory directives.

Institutional Legacy

Rooted in NES values, serving the Malnad region

National Education Society was founded by visionary freedom fighters and philanthropists including Sri H. S. Rudrappa, Sri S. V. Krishna Murthy Rao, Sri S. R. Nagappa Setty, and Sri D. S. Dinakar. Their commitment was carried forward by leaders such as Sri Girimaji N. Rajagopal and Sri D. R. Ratnakar.

75+ years

NES educational reach

NES has expanded to 36 institutions across Engineering, Science, Pharmacy, Commerce, Humanities, Education, Law, Business Administration, Physical Education, Computer Applications, and Applied Sciences.

Rural focus

Access and affordability

The society continues to work from its mission of providing quality education at reasonable cost, with special focus on rural and women's education.

New initiatives

Expanding education

Campus Life

A 39.4 acre learning environment

The green campus supports more than 4,500 students with spacious classrooms, modern laboratories, workshops, computing facilities, a comprehensive library, hostels, sports facilities, and everyday student amenities.

Student Living

Hostels accommodate approximately 600 boys and 450 girls, supported by a cafeteria, co-operative society, bank, post office, guesthouse, and medical centre.

Sports Infrastructure

The campus includes a multi-gym, turf cricket pitch, modern playground, and a KSCA-standard pitch with pavilion that has hosted Ranji Trophy and state-level tournaments.

Sustainability

Rainwater harvesting and a sewage treatment plant support water conservation, recycling, and pollution control across the campus.

Research and Innovation

Research-led learning with industry exposure

Faculty members have presented and published more than 500 research papers in national and international journals, conferences, and seminars. The institution has completed funded projects worth over Rs. 30 lakhs and supports innovation through JNNRIC, JNNCART, and the New Age Incubation Centre.

JNNCE hosts national and international conferences, academic events, seminars, technical talks, Techzone, and JANVEY, building a strong culture of learning beyond the classroom.

Industry and Alumni Network

JNNCE has signed MoUs with more than 10 organizations including L&T Edu Tech, Unimech Aerospace, Ultra Tech Cement, and Qualitas Technologies, with additional academic collaboration through IIT Dharwad and Subbaiah Institute of Medical Science.

Its alumni community of more than 20,000 members contributes across industry, research, academics, and government organizations in India and abroad.
